Riyadh is the capital city of Saudi Arabia, located in the central region of the country. With a population of over 7 million people, it is the largest city in the Kingdom and one of the fastest-growing cities in the world. Riyadh is the political and administrative center of Saudi Arabia, serving as the headquarters of the Saudi government and home to many important institutions, including the King Abdulaziz City for Science and Technology and the Diplomatic Quarter. The city is also a major commercial hub, with a thriving economy based on industries such as finance, technology, and telecommunications. Riyadh is known for its impressive modern architecture, including the Kingdom Centre, a skyscraper that is one of the tallest buildings in the country, as well as its numerous parks and cultural attractions.
