Medinah is a city in the Hejaz region of Saudi Arabia. It is located approximately 420 kilometers north of the city of Mecca and is considered the second holiest city in Islam after Mecca. The city is also known as Medina or Madinah, and its full name is Al-Madinah Al-Munawwarah, which means "the enlightened city" or "the radiant city."
